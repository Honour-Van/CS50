                                    【独家！#CGTN专访武汉病毒研究所研究员石正丽# 之二：动物感染实验证明此前分离出的冠状病毒是造成武汉不明原因肺炎的一个病原】病原鉴定的工作有一个重要环节，叫做动物感染实验。2月6号和2月9号，我们分别完成了转基因小鼠的动物感染实验、恒河猴的动物感染实验。这两个动物感染实验都证明我们分离出来的冠状病毒是造成武汉不明原因肺炎的一个病原。"The work we did earlier is only part of the pathogen identification process, in which we can learn its genetic information and what kind of virus it is," said Shi Zhengli, a Chinese virologist based at the Wuhan Institute of Virology. She also stressed that animal infection experiments are another important step in pathogen identification. “These experiments are actually part of what's called Koch's postulates. Only through animal infection experiments can we finally determine that a certain pathogen is the main cause of a certain disease,” she explained, adding that animal infection experiments conducted on February 6 and 9 proved that the coronavirus she and her team isolated were the cause of the unknown pneumonia.
